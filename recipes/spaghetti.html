Spaghetti

Description

A delicious pasta dish to be enjoyed by the whole family!

A quick meal for that can be prepared in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of noodles
  • 1 can of your favorite spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 cup of garlic powder
  • Grated parmesan and romano cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles in a pot filled with water. Feel free to add salt to aid in boiling and add a tad more flavor to noodles
  2. Heat sauce. While noodles are boiling add your sauce to a small pot and heat on the stove. Be careful not to overheat the sauce.
  3. Strain noodles and enjoy. Pour the sauce over the noodles and sprinkle some parmesan and romano over the top.
