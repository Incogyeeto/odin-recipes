Hot Wings

Description

A nice spicy appetizer!

Perfect to take to roadtrips, picnics, etc!

Can be enjoyed hot or cold!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken wings (drums or flats)
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of frying oil of your choice
  • 1/4 cup of hot sauce
  • 1/2 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Coat wings with flour, oil, and spice mixture
  2. Make the sauce in a saucepan on medium heat. Combine butter and hot sauce, stir until butter is melted.
  3. Fry the wings in a skillet filled with oil on 375*F until a golden brown crust is formed.
  4. Coat the wings. Drizzle the hot sauce over the wings, or place in a sealed bowl and toss them if you like.
