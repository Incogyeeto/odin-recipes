Chili

Description

This chili dish is great with cornbread or corn chips!

A great soup option for those who like a little spice in their life!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of ground beef (can be substituted with ground turkey)
  • Minced onion
  • 1 Can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Can of Kidney Beans (spicy kidney beans if you like spicy)
  • 1 Bag of Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
