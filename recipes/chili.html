Chili

Description

This chili dish is great with cornbread or corn chips!

A great soup option for those who like a little spice in their life!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of ground beef (can be substituted with ground turkey)
  • Minced onion
  • 1 Can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Can of Kidney Beans (spicy kidney beans if you like spicy)
  • 1 Bag of Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the Beef. Feel free to add the minced onion to the beef (or turkey) while browning
  2. Add all Remaining Ingredients. Start with the tomato sauce and then mix everything else in. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Reduce Heat and Let Simmer. That's it! Enjoy your homemade chili with cornbread, chips, or saltine crackers.
